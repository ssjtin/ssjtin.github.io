Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find the answer to your question in our FAQs below:

  • How do I save my favorite jokes, facts, and riddles?
    Simply tap the "Save" button under the item you want to add to your favorites library.
  • How do I access my saved favorites?
    Go to the "Library" section in the app to view all your saved jokes, facts, and riddles.
  • Why am I seeing ads?
    We use AdMob to display ads in our app to keep it free for all users. Ads help support the development and maintenance of the app.
  • Is my data safe?
    Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more information.
