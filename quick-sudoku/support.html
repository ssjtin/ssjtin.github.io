Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I change the difficulty level?

A: From the main menu, select the difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard, Insane) before starting a new game.

Q: How do I use the pencil mode?

A: Tap on the pencil icon while playing to enter pencil mode. You can then make notes in the cells without committing to a final answer.

Q: How do I undo a move?

A: Tap the undo button to revert your last move. You can use the undo function as many times as needed.
