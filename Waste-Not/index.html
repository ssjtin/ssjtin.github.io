About Waste Not

Tired of discovering expired food in your pantry or fridge? Waste Not is here to help! This easy-to-use app lets you track the expiry dates of your goods and ensures you never miss an important date again. Stay on top of your groceries and household items with timely notifications and home screen widgets for quick glances.

Features

  • Expiry Date Tracking: Keep track of the expiry dates for all your food and household items. Waste Not helps you stay organized.
  • Local Notifications: Set custom reminders and receive notifications before items expire. Choose how many days in advance you'd like to be notified.
  • Widgets: Access your tracked items and their expiry statuses directly from your home screen for quick reference.
